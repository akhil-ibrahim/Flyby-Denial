# Our Team 🧬

Meet the researchers behind the Flyby-Denial bioinformatics project at the University of Miami.

---

## Akhil Ibrahim
**Role:** Student Immunology Researcher  
Akhil is an undergraduate senior studying microbiology and immunology. His work focuses on investigating T cell function using wet lab approaches, aiming to bridge experimental immunology with computational methods for integrative bioinformatics.

## Ngoc Tao
**Role:** Student Researcher  
Ngoc is an undergraduate researcher with a focus on cancer metabolism and immunotherapy. She explores how molecular and immune mechanisms intersect in disease progression to contribute to advancements in cancer-related treatment.

## Kennedy Kreutzer
**Role:** Student Researcher  
Kennedy is an undergraduate biomedical engineering major and a researcher at the Miami Project to Cure Paralysis. Her work focuses on advancing research related to spinal cord injury and developing translational medical solutions.
